PHYS 226: Elementary Particle Physics

Course Description

Assignments (there may be some mistakes, so use at your own risk):

  • Deep Inelastic Scattering, Hadronic Cross Sections, Nuclear Structure, Running of the Coupling, Renormalization Schemes
  • Grand Unification Scale, Bjorken Scaling Violation, Nuclear Pion Condensate, DGLAP and Splitting Functions
  • Lattice QCD, Color Attractive/Repulsive Forces, 1/N Expansion, Axial Anomalies, Instantons in QM and QCD, Theta Vacuum
  • Lagrangian Mechanics, Quantum Fields, Young Tableau
  • Chiral, O(N) and SU(2) Symmetry Breaking
  • Sigma Model, Non-Abelian Gauge Transformations, SU(3) Symmetry Breaking
  • Electroweak Unification, B-L Anomalies
  • GIM Mechanism, C and CP Violation, CKM Matrix, Invisible Width of the Z
  • Charged Lepton Flavor Violation Presentation
  • Units, Kinematics, Distributions, Diagrams
  • Decay Rates, Branching Ratios, Discrete Symmetries
  • Bound States
  • Gauge Bosons, Cross Sections
  • Tau Lepton, Deep Inelastic Scattering, Dark Matter
  • Proton Decay, Neutrino Oscillations, LBNF and SNO
  • Higgs, SUSY, Atomic/Nuclear Properties of Materials
