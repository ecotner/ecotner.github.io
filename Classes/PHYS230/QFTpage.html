PHYS 230: Quantum Field Theory

Course Description

Assignments

  • Nonperturbative Pair Production, Instantons, Wilson Lines
  • Nonabelian Feynman Rules, SU(3) and Lorentz Group Lie Algebras, BRST Symmetry
  • Gell-Mann Levy Sigma Model
  • Bosonization of 1+1 Dim Fermions, Chiral Anomaly using Pauli-Villars Regulation
  • Gauge Invariance and Moller Scattering
  • phi^4 and Yukawa Theory at 1 Loop
  • Electron Mass Renormalization and Spectral Representations
  • Classical Field Theory
  • Interacting Scalar Field Theory
  • Path Integrals, Dirac Equation
  • Interacting Fermionic Field Theory and Scattering
  • Electron Magnetic Moment
